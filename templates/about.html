About SignSpeak

SignSpeak is designed to make conversations between sign language users and non‑signers effortless and instant. By recognizing hand gestures and turning them into spoken and written words, we make communication more inclusive for everyone.

This project is brought to you by ByteBusters — a team passionate about removing communication barriers and creating tech that’s simple, intuitive, and accessible.

From smooth translations to a friendly interface, every feature in SignSpeak is made with the goal of connecting people.
